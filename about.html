Basic information on triangles

A triangle has three sides and three angles.
The size of the angles and the length of sides helps in determining the type of a triangle.
For today, we will only cover the types of triangles as can be derived from the length of the sides.

Types of Triangles

  • Isosceles Triangle

    • This is a triangle with only two sides equal.

  • Equilateral

    • This is a triangle with all sides equal.

  • Scalene

    • This a triangle with no sides equal.
